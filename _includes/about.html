About


Kristina Vilyams is a multidisciplinary UI/UX with expertise in graphic design, Augmented Reality, and 3D modeling. Known for her pioneering work in creating innovative try-on AR filters and has collaborated with renowned fashion brands and artists, including Susan Alexandra, Dollchunk, Gustaf Westman and ThugPop. Has been featured in Architectural Digest and recognized for her innovative design on Lenslist.
