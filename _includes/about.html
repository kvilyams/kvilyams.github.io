About


Kristina Vilyams is an multidisciplinary UI/UX and Product Designer with over 5 years of experience in the industry. Her passion lies in creating innovative digital experiences at the intersection of design, fashion, art, and performance.
With skills in graphic design, augmented reality, 3D modeling, motion graphics, and marketing, she have been involved in exciting projects, including creating try-on augmented reality filters for renowned fashion brands such as Susan Alexandra, Dollchunk, ThugPop, Gustaf Westman Objects, Ouhlala Le Shop, and Zona Johna.
