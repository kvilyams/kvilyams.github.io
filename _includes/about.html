About


Kristina Vilyams is a multidisciplinary UI/UX and product designer with expertise in graphic design, Augmented Reality, and 3D modeling. She is widely known for her pioneering work in creating innovative try-on AR filters and has collaborated with renowned fashion brands and artists, including Susan Alexandra, Dollchunk, Gustaf Westman, ThugPop, Zona Johna and Ouhlala Le Shop. Kristina's work has been featured in Architectural Digest and she has been recognized for her innovative design on Lenslist.
